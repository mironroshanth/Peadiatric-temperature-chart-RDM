1.Record live temperatures


  • What is a 'Record live temperatures' chart?

    • A Record live temperatures chart is an online fever chart created by me to record the body temperatures of a person, then and there. The speciality of this chart is that it can mark temperatures at your present time when you input the data (temperatures).



    • There won't be any date or time inputs in the above page. The page will mark the temperatures automatically according to the time the temperatures are input.

      • Note:
        Note that the chart in this version uses the Indian Standered Time(IST). Which is GMT+05:30.


2.Record fever history


  • Why use a 'Record fever history' chart?

    • We can use it to record a series of body temperatures of a person. You can record it at any date and input any temperatures recorded at any day.



    • This chart has advanced options such as,

      • You shall be able to input the date the temperatures were measured from.
      • You would be able to mark the,
          -Date
          -Time
         precisely and manually, along with the temperature you input.
      • The data spacing wll be based on date an time inputs.

    • Wih many options it wil be more user friendly.
