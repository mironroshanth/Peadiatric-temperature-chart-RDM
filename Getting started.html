How to use


This website is used to mark the temperatures of a child. The methods of using the app are explained below.


When the mouse point touches the point styling, a label will pop up as shown in the figure above. Initially, the chart would be marked with the normal body temperature of children. When you click on that label, the page will ask you the temperature recorded at the mentioned time.



All you have to do to mark the temperature on the chart is to enter enter the temperature recorded at the mentioned time. Note that the temperature you enter is in fahrenheit.If it is in celsius click here to convert to fahrenheit.
